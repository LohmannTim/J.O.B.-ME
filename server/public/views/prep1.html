Prepare- Advice from a pro.

Let's get start with some action items, a few insider tips, and a bit of homework to get yourself prepared.

Scrub your social media of anything you wouldn't want your employer to see.

Post recommendations from previous employers on your LinkedIn account

Take a deep breath and understand this is a process that takes time.

When applying to a position, always be very thorough as applicants are often passed over for typos or missed information.

Error on the side of over applying even if you don't meet all or even most of the "requirements."

If you aren't excited about the positiono you are applying for, the company probably won't be excited about you.

Dress as if you are showing up for your first day of work and you have a lunch meeting with the CEO.

Read up on your audience, learn their background, and find common interests.
