Execute- My Questions!

  • While I have a high level understanding of what the company does, will you please share more with me about how you and your team fit in to the organization?
  • What are the greatest challenges your team faces in the coming months/years?
  • Why have you chosen to add another team member?
  • In what areas is your team struggling and/or needs the most support?
  • What deliverable are you and your team responsible for?
  • If you chose not to hire for this position, what would be the consequences?
  • What is your plan for the next six months, year, five years?
  • Have I answered all of your questions adequately, or would you like for me to clarify any points to you?
